What is Cyber Security?



    As defined by the NCSC, Cyber Security is how individuals and organisations reduce the risk of cyber attack. The 21st century has one of the fastest developments in computing, such as the iPhone, 3D printing and Augmented Reality. These developed technologies have created a more accessible lifestyle for the average person. More and more computer scientists are entering the field and day by day research with Computer Science grows at an increasing rate. However, with high growth in the number of electronic devices being produced and used, data protection and security will certaintly be more important. Cyber Security is one of the fields in Computing, which seeks to protect data of Internet users, including businesses (large and small) and individuals. Therefore, its role will be more essential because of the rise in online banking and shopping, where individuals personal details can be potentially exposed to malicious hackers. Thus there is ongoing research on this topic, and many technologies have been developed already, such as Encryption.
